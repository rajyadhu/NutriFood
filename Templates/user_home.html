Frequently Asked Questions

What is NutriFood?

NutriFood is a platform that allows users to create personalized diet plans and perform image recognition on food images.

How can I create a diet plan?

To create a diet plan, simply select your desired food items from our menu and organize them into meals based on your dietary needs.

How does image recognition work?

Our image recognition feature utilizes deep learning algorithms to analyze food images and predict the name of the food depicted in the image.
